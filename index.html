About

About Us

SIPMS

At our Paramedical College, we pride ourselves on offering comprehensive and hands-on training in various disciplines. Our experienced faculty members are dedicated to providing a supportive learning environment where students can thrive and develop both professionally and personally. We are committed to staying at the forefront of advancements in the healthcare industry, ensuring that our curriculum is up-to-date and relevant to current practices and technologies. Through a combination of classroom instruction, practical training, and clinical experience, our students gain the practical skills and theoretical knowledge necessary to succeed in their chosen field. In addition to academic excellence, we also emphasize the importance of character development and ethical behavior. Our students are encouraged to embody compassion, integrity, and professionalism in their interactions with patients and colleagues. Whether you aspire to become a medical laboratory technician, radiologic technologist, or respiratory therapist, our paramedical college is here to support your journey towards a rewarding and fulfilling career in healthcare. Join us and embark on a path of discovery, growth, and service to others.

Affilated From: Uttar Pradesh State Medical Faculty

We Offers

Diploma in Physiotherapy

12th Science | 60+

Our Diploma in Physiotherapy program is designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and clinical experience necessary to excel in the field of physiotherapy.

Whether you're passionate about helping others recover from injuries, improving mobility, or enhancing overall wellness, our program offers a solid foundation for your career.

Diploma in Optometry

12th Science | 60+

Our Diploma in Optometry program is designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and clinical experience necessary to excel in the field of optometry.

Whether you're passionate about improving vision, diagnosing eye conditions, or helping others maintain eye health, our program offers a solid foundation for your career.

LABS

Our institution boasts state-of-the-art laboratories equipped with all the latest machines and equipment necessary for our Physiotherapy and Optometry courses, providing students with hands-on experience and practical skills essential for their future careers.

FACULTY
